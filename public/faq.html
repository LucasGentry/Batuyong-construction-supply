General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at ZetaWork is only required if you will be working. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

What is needed to register?

Workers and buyers need a valid paypal address (verified) address to register. This is to ensure the safety between parties.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All transaction on the website uses point system. Workers get rewarded with point for every task submitted correctly and penalized when workers submitted incomplete/poorly written/copied.
Workers

What task should worker do?

Any user can work on any task posted. As long as the "Submit Work" button is active. Sign Up task should be prioritized.

How many points can I earn per task?

There is no limit on how much you can earn. The reward varies per task. Task reward ranges from 5points to 2000 points depending on the quality and intensity of the task.

What is Proof of Work/Proof Required?

To ensure that workers are not submitting/copying the works of other worker. A worker needs to submit a proof of work as a valid proof that the worker done the task successfully. If in a situation that the proof submitted in the past will be submitted in the future. The first entry will be considered.

How to submit my proof of work?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the WORK section and click "Submit Work".
  • Read the Job Description and Proof Required.
  • Enter the proof required.
  • Enter the Captcha and Submit. The captcha should be type in lowercase.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal or BTC. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 100 USD.

Why workers points are decreasing?

To ensure that workers submit high quality work. Workers who are submitting poorly written content/copied/incomplete task will be penalized accordingly. A deduction of 10% of the task reward will be deducted to the worker.

Why are the reason why points are decreasing?

Workers who are submitting copied content/poorly written content/incomplete content are the main causes of points reduction. To avoid this, All you need to do is:
  • Read the proof required and supply what is needed
  • Follow the Subject-Verb-Agreement when writing your proof of work.
  • Always submit proof of work in the form of paragraphs. Any proof written with bullet, number, list will be penalized.
  • Always follow numbering. If a proof requires 3 proofs.
  • Follow the example provided. Each task have a proof required sample. The sample should look like your submission.

Why are common mistake of workers and end up getting a penalty point?

Here is a common mistake done by other workers:
  • "i", this should always be capitalize.
  • Spun contents, when we feel that the proof submitted is a spun content.
  • Proper choose of words, spelling and punctuations.
  • No numbering on proof of work submitted, incomplete proof, duplicate or copied.
  • Use of foul words.
  • Use of words such as "hahahah", "hihihi" ,too many emoticon.
  • Not following word counts. Each task have different word requirements.

Will worker be paid if they write too much for a given task?

No, If a task requires 500 words and a worker provided 800 words that "proof of work" will be marked as invalid. The maximum words should be +10% of the word required. For example: 500 words * 0.10 = 50 words. Thus, 500 words + 50 words is equal to 550 words which is the maximum amount of words. If it exceeds it will be mark as invalid.

How to compute my earnings in USD?

Here is the rate per user:
  • User with less than 1,000 proof submitted : 1USD per 1,000 points
  • User with less than 1,001-10,000 proof submitted : 1.5USD per 1000 points
  • User with less than 10,001-50,000 proof submitted : 2USD per 1000 points
  • User with less than 50,001-100,000 proof submitted : 3USD per 1000 points
  • User with less than 100,001 upwards proof submitted : 5USD per 1000 points
